Campus Life

Front Office

Established In 1974

The front office is the closest actual building to the front gate. This is where visitors can check in, where the sick bay is, and where to go if you're late.



Library

Established In 1986

The library is where students can borrow books, return books, and read. There are hundreds (if not thousands) of books here, and it is open at the second half of lunchtime.

Inter-School PSSA Event

State Champions of Inter-School PSSA

An amazing feat for both the school and the students. This is a very hard thing to do, and we are very proud of our students.

What's new

  • Handball Courts

    Since the old handball courts have started to fade out, the Upper Quad needs a touchup. So these newly-painted handball courts have been painted. Both making the UQ a more fun place to play on, and giving the bland area of tarmac and concrete a touch of colour, these courts are an amazing addition to thr UQ.
